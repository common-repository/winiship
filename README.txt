== Description ==

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rGWR04C_ZMc

Official WordPress Plugin for WiniShip. 

WiniShip integrates with WooCommerce stores to provide a dropshipping solution for your customers.

Find amazing products to sell and we'll handle the fulfillment of the order automatically.

Save time and focus on marketing to expand your e-commerce business.

Browse products located in the EU and US.

Platform Overview: 
1. Import products from WiniShip
2. After importing, the products will appear on your WooCommerce store
3. When someone orders that product, we handle the fulfillment automatically
4. You pay for the listing price and keep the profit from the sale

Get started for free on winiship.com

== Installation ==

Once you've installed the plugin, activate it.

Then, you'll need to create an account on winiship.com and connect your WooCommerce store to WiniShip.

Step 1
Connect your WooCommerce store to WiniShip.

Step 2
Go to the dashboard on winiship.com, select "Stores" and then select "settings" in the top left corner.

Step 3
On the settings page you'll be able to copy your API key which you'll need for the next step.

Step 4
1. After you have you copied your API key from winiship.com, paste the value into the WiniShip plugin.
